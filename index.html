Meet Matt

Hello All, thanks again for coming to visit our site. I’m happy to see that you’ve made it this far. Here I will tell you a little bit of myself. I was born St. Paul, Minnesota in the year of 1981, and was a good looking baby.  In my free time I try to stay healthy by eating right and exercising. I also play World of Warcraft. In the winter months I love to snowboard, and in the summer months you will likely find my in or near the water.

Life was tough for me in my early years up to after I graduated high school. I didn’t have a lot of friends and struggled in school. When I turned 30 I decided that if I wanted a life I could be proud of I needed to get a degree in my desired profession, Web Development.

From there I enrolled at Rasmussen College in their Information Technology program with a major in Web Development. I choose Rasmussen because they offer 100% online courses which allowed me to continue working full-time at my CNC machinist job. I graduated in December 2014 with an Associate’s Degree at the top of my class with a 3.98 GPA, which is my proudest accomplishment in my life. Regardless of where my career leads me I would like to pursue a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science.

After I got my degree I did struggle to find a job, and after a lot of hard work and looking I got a job at EasyITGuys as their first web developer/administrator. My main objective there is to build and maintain WordPress sites. I do that be using the best plugins and themes to keep the look of the sites looking modern and awesome. I make sure that all our software, plugins, themes, and core WordPress stay up-to-date and trouble shoot any issues that arise. I also stay in direct contact with the EasyITGuys clients to make sure that they are satisfied with their website(s). Currently, I am managing 25 WordPress websites.

After 9 months of being there they have already hired other web developers/interns that I am in charge of training and make sure they stay busy. I also do some work with our dedicated server and Office 365.

Though I really enjoying working with WordPress I also love to code custom sites. I want to become an expert in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, and keep up on all the new technologies. In short, my goal is to become the best front end web developer I can be.
